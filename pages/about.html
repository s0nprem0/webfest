About Elite League of Information Technology Students

Organization Description

ELITS, the Elite League of Information Technology Students, represents a vibrant and influential school organization within the university. Comprising a diverse group of motivated IT students across different academic years, ELITS embodies the credibility of student engagement and leadership. Operating within a democratic framework, ELITS boasts a structured committee, with elected positions such as President, Vice Presidents, Secretaries, Treasurer, Auditor, Business Manager, GAD Representative, P.R.O, and Representatives. This dynamic organization plays a crucial role in fostering a tech-savvy community, aligning with the university's IT-driven educational environment. As we embark on a new academic year, it is with great pleasure that we introduce our dedicated team of school officers for the year ahead. These individuals have been selected to lead and serve our school community, and they bring with them a commitment to excellence and a passion for making this year memorable and productive for all of us.

Core Values

Elite League of Information Technology Students embodies a set of core values that guide their actions and shape their purpose. Assuring overall competency in all I.T disciplines such as ensuring the confidentiality and integrity of data while upholding a strong commitment to cybersecurity. Promoting teamwork and cooperation with other departments to align technology with the university's goals. Giving top priority to technological solutions that improve the learning and research experiences for students and faculty. Being flexible and responsive to shifting technological trends and the changing needs of the university community. Assuming responsibility for technology decisions and investments and their effects on the university's mission and resources. Striking for ongoing improvements and optimizations in IT services and infrastructure.
