    • PREAMBLE

    We, the members of the College of Engineering and Information Technology Student Council, solemnly swear that by this organization, we shall promote extra-curricular excellence in the fields of Agricultural and Food Engineering, Architecture, Civil Engineering, Computer and Electronics Engineering, Industrial Engineering and Technology, and Information Technology, facilitate values formation, and develop a harmonious relationship among the members of the College, and for the success of our undertakings and prosperity, do ordain and promulgate this constitution.

    OBJECTIVE

    CBL Article V, Section 1 and 2

    The Council shall promote extra-curricular excellence in the fields of Agricultural and Food Engineering, Architecture, Civil Engineering, Computer and Electronics Engineering, Industrial Engineering and Technology, and Information Technology

    The council shall

    • Aid in the development and enhancement of students' skills and potentials;
    • Aid in exposing the students to various civic and welfare extension activities;
    • Foster camaraderie among its members;
    • Facilitate values formation; and
    • Represent and unify the students of College of Engineering and Information Technology Student Council Cavite State University - Don Severino de las Alas Campus.
